Strain Information

What does "Feminized" mean?

Feminized seeds are bred to produce only female plants, which are the ones that produce the buds you might be interested in. This eliminates the need to identify and remove male plants.

What's the difference between Photoperiod and Autoflowering strains?

Photoperiod plants depend on light cycles to change from vegetative to flowering stages, while Autoflowering strains automatically switch to flowering after a certain period regardless of light exposure.
