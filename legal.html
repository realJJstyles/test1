Legal Status of Cannabis Seeds

Cannabis seeds are legally considered a novelty or souvenir item in many jurisdictions. Here's a general guide:

  • USA: Legal status varies by state. Always check local laws before purchasing or using seeds.
  • Canada: Legal for adults, but cultivation must comply with local laws.
  • Europe: Legal for collection in many countries, but cultivation legality varies.
  • Other Regions: Please research your local regulations as they can differ significantly.
